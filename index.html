Our Services

We Offer

Basic Health Services

We provide essential health services at the community level to ensure the well-being of individuals.

Education & Development

We promote education, address social issues, and provide opportunities for personal and professional growth among youth.

Rehabilitation & Support

We work towards the rehabilitation of Persons with Disabilities (PWDs) and provide assistance to individuals affected by substance abuse.

Female Education

We are committed to promoting female education in rural areas, ensuring equal access to knowledge and opportunities.

Child Abuse Prevention

We actively work towards raising awareness about child abuse and creating platforms to address and prevent it.

Youth Empowerment

We provide opportunities for the youth to learn the teachings of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) and receive mental, intellectual, academic, and practical training.
