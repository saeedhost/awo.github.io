Our Mission and Vision

About Us

Our mission is to empower communities by providing comprehensive health services, promoting education and awareness, and addressing social issues. We strive to improve the lives of individuals, particularly the marginalized and underprivileged, by offering opportunities for personal growth and development.

Our vision is to create a society where every individual has access to quality healthcare, equal educational opportunities, and a supportive environment free from discrimination. We envision a future where communities thrive through holistic development, empowered youth contribute to nation-building, and societal prejudices are dismantled. Together, we aim to foster a healthier, more inclusive, and prosperous nation for all.

Aim & Objectives

Our Aim and Objectives

  1. To initiate measures for the provision of basic health services at the community level
  2. To work on humanities.
  3. To work on Rehabilitation for PWD's and drugs benefit of all Communities in all over the Country
  4. To work on female education.in rural Areas of whole district as well.
  5. To work on child abuse in every plateform.
  6. To make the youth aware of the teachings of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) and to provide them opportunities for mental, intellectual, academic and practical training.
  7. To make practical efforts to provide bread, cloth, housing, etc. to the distressed people in disaster-affected, or flood-affected areas
  8. To provide Health first aids in the society.
  9. To provide opportunities for modern sciences along with religious education to the youth of the nation, so that experts in various sciences can be provided to the nation.
  10. Eliminating social, linguistic and sectarian prejudices from the society through religious and contemporary sciences.
  11. To give opportunities of religious and modern education to the youth of the backward section of the society, so that such unemployed people can be available to the society, who can lead to the upliftment of the country and the nation.
